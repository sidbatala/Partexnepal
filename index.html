About PARTEX NEPAL

PARTEX NEPAL is a Kathmandu-based company specializing in the production of high-quality tissue products. Our mission is to deliver innovative and sustainable hygiene solutions that enhance the lives of our customers.

We are committed to quality, affordability, and environmental responsibility.

Message from Chairman

"As the Chairman of PARTEX NEPAL, I am proud to lead a company that is dedicated to providing high-quality tissue products to our customers. Our commitment to innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction drives everything we do. We strive to make a positive impact on the lives of our customers and the environment."

Rajesh Shrestha
Chairman, PARTEX NEPAL
